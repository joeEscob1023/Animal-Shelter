About

Furry Friend Rescue is your local Las Vegas stop to adopt animals! We are a no kill shelter and we make sure all our animals are ready to take home. Unlike other shelters, we have our animals interact with their species through out the day so when they are taken home, they are already used to being around other dogs, cats, ect. We have a 24/7, 365 day a year staff that is helping animals either recover from abuse, a sickness, an injury, or just playing with them all day. We are proud to say that we are a top 5 choice in Las Vegas to adopt pets. Please look through our animals and if you see one you like, press the adopt star so we can send an email will list the times and days we have available.
